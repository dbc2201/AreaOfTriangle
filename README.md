Problem Statement
---

Calculate the area of a triangle using [Heron's Formula](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Heron's_formula)

$$area=\sqrt{s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c)}$$

$$s=\frac{a+b+c}{2}$$

Where `a`, `b`, and `c` are the lengths of the sides of the triangle.
